THE MERCURY.


Mercury is the closest planet to the sun and the eighth largest. It has a diameter of 4,880 kilometers. Mercury has the widest temperature extremes in the solar system ranging from 90 degrees Kelvin to 700 degrees Kelvin. Mercury has a thick iron core and a thinner outer crust of rocky material.It has a solid surface that is covered with craters like our Moon. It has a thin atmosphere, and it doesn't have any moons.
