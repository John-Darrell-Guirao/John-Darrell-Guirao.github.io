TOP 5 CYRPTO GAMES

1. Town Star

The main goal of Town Star is to manufacture things that you can then sell to nearby cities. When you do this, your rank will rise, and the higher your rank rises, the more in-game money you'll need to upgrade your farm. This means that the wealthier you become, the more difficult the game becomes, but the higher the benefits you will receive as a result. This entire experience is completely free to play.

Town Star is a blockchain-based play-to-earn (P2E) game created by Gala Games, whose staff also co-founded Zynga Games. Players are given the option to create their own Town and produce resources in order to earn the Town Star (TOWN) token, which is the game's native cryptocurrency token.

Games have traditionally locked down all of the assets obtained within the games, meaning that anyone attempting to assign real-world value to the things would be banned. To put it another way, anything you gain in a typical game is tied to that single firm and your account. This means that innumerable players have lost assets throughout the years as a result of illegal attempts to profit from selling them.

Gala Games set out to create a world where gamers could do just that, selling their in-game assets in the form of a non-fungible token (NFT) on marketplaces.
