Tezos XTZ

Tezos is a decentralized computing platform and cryptocurrency. Proof of stake consensus, formal verification (which allows developers to verify the validity of their code), and the opportunity for stakeholders to vote on protocol changes are among its characteristics. Baking is the process of creating and validating blocks in Tezos. Tezos token holders who stake their tokens can obtain Tezos tokens as a compensation for making and verifying blocks.

Tezos is the foundation for the internet's future, with user engagement and governance at its core. Without the use of middlemen, users can engage directly and frictionlessly with one another through a decentralized network, exchanging value and connecting with various apps. Despite these challenges, the cryptocurrency was fast gaining traction. XTZ has been listed on over ten major crypto exchanges, including Changelly, two years after its debut.
