2. William Shatner’s personal memorabilia

William Shatner's quest to discover weird new worlds continues with the release of items from his personal album on the WAX Blockchain. On the WAX blockchain, Mr. Shatner is releasing his nonfungible token (NFT) collection for admirers all around the world to acquire and sell. Some of the treasures he's digitized for this release have never been viewed by the general public before!

Fans and collectors will have the chance to win a golden souvenir, which they may exchange for a real-life autographed headshot of the actor. A real-life autographed action figure of William Shatner will be given away to one lucky person.

Intimate images from Mr. Shatner's personal life and cinematic career, including a candid image of him hugging "Star Trek" co-star Leonard Nimoy, will be included in this rare collection of Mr. Shatner's most personal artifacts.

Every facet of every collectable transaction is recorded and stored in the blockchain. These blockchain records cannot be altered and can be viewed by anybody. As a result, the blockchain serves as confirmation that the collectibles are genuine, and they may be traded or sold at any moment without taking a chance with a buyer or seller because the WAX Blockchain verifies everything.
