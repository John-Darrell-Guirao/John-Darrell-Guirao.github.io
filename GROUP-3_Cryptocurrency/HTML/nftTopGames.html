Top 5 Crypto Games

You should know

1. Town Star

Town Star is a blockchain-based play-to-earn (P2E) game created by Gala Games, whose staff also co-founded Zynga Games. Players are given the option to create their own Town and produce resources in order to earn the Town Star (TOWN) token, which is the game's native cryptocurrency token.

2. Axie Infinity

Axie Infinity is a blockchain-based game in which players buy NFTs of attractive creatures and then battle them. A “play-to-earn” movement has emerged around the game, with players joining scholarship programs and academies.

3. Illuvium

Illuvium is an Ethereum-based blockchain app that allows the Unchanging X blockchain and Layer-2 scalability technologies.

4. Guild of Guardians

Guild of Guardians is a mobile RPG where players can turn their gaming passion into assets. It will be a multiplayer, fantasy, action RPG where players build their dream team of ‘Guardians’ and compete in a guild to earn epic, tradeable rewards.s

5. Spider Tanks

Spider Tanks is a new crypto-based multiplayer brawler with a lot of action. Spider Tanks, unlike standard MOBAs (Multiplayer Online Battle Arenas), uses the Ethereum blockchain to allow users to earn money while they play. This Spider Tank review goes into much detail.
