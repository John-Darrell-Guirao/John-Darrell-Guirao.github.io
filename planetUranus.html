THE URANUS.


It was the first planet found with the aid of a telescope, Uranus was discovered in 1781 by astronomer William Herschel, although he originally thought it was either a comet or a star. It is also the third-largest in terms of size, the fourth-largest in terms of mass, and one of the least dense objects in our Solar System. It can also be simply described as the planet, is mostly swirling fluids.
