Sweden

Sverige

As I've mentioned form the other future destinations that I've wanted to go, beautiful sceneries would always have a place in my heart. That is why I would want to see the Northern Lights myself in Sweden and experience the wonders of nature and science itself. The second destination would be the Vasa Museum in Stockholm. I was also fascinated with their nordic culture and traditions particularly about the Vikings which I would be able to know more when I visit this place. Lastly, the place I would want to visit is the Göta Canal, where I would ride a boat and experience the so-called "Sweden's greatest feat of engineering" first-hand.
