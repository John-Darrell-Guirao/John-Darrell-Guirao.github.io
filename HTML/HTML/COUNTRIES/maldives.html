Maldives

Treasure Island

One of the countries that I want to visit would be, you guessed it, Maldives. I just love how everything about it screams "this is a relaxing place". I think wherever you go here, you would still be able to have a great time. Nonetheless, the first place I would visit here would be the Banana Reef, where you would be able to have one of the great diving experience of your life. I just simply love how rich the marine life here is in Maldives, and I would want to see it by my own eyes. In correlation to this, I would want to visit the Maldive Victory, where they described this place as the best diving spot which features a sunken ship that you could explore. Lastly would be the Kurumba Beach, there is no particular reason. I just love the vibe of tranquility that this place emits.
