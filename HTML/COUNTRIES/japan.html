Japan

The Land of the Rising Sun

Japan, the country that I'm dreaming of to travel ever since I was a child. I am and will always be fascinated with the Japanese culture, traditions, folk lores, etc. The first travel spot I would definitely go to would be Akihabara. As an avid fan of anime, Akihabara for me would be my personal heaven. I've always dreamt of shopping there, read a manga from a manga store, experience the maid cafes, and many more. The second destination would be Mt. Fuji, it may not look like it but I've always admired and will always appreciate beautiful sceneries to look at. Lastly, I would want to visit the Himeji Castle. One of the things that I've kept an interest into are Japanese castles. For me, how they were structured and built would always look graceful and sophisticated to me.
