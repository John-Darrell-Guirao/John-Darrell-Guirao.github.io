The Philippines

Pearl of the Orient Seas

Before I travel outside the country, I would still want to know and see what our country has to offer. Since my family is not a fan of travelling, there are still so many places here in the Philippines that I've yet still to see and experience. First from the list would be of course, Boracay. Since I was a child, I've always wanted to touch the sandy beach and be mesmerized by lovely sunset that Boracay offers to many locals and foreigners, love and appreciate. Second thing to do here would be the island hopping. I've always loved travelling, whether it be by land, sea, or air, visiting and experiencing new places and sceneries would never bore the life out of me. Lastly, what I would want to visit would be the Crystal Cove, where it is clad with rock formations, cliffs, and hollowed coves. This is also a good spot for snorkeling, which I've not yet experienced as well.
