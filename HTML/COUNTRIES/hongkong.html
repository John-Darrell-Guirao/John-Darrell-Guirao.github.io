Hong Kong

Fragrant Harbour

Hong Kong, one of the first countries that I've visited so far in my life. My family does not really appreciate the wonders of travelling however if given a choice, Hongkong would be one of the top 5 countries that I would want to visit. As a person who seeks thrill and adventure, a Hong Kong trip wouldn't be complete if I would not be able to visit Disneyland again. I could still remember the unforgettable fun experiences I had riding those rides and I would do anything just to experience it once again. The second place I would want to visit there is the Victoria Peak, where I would be able to view the majestic view of Hong Kong from a top and far place. Lastly, is to ride a star ferry. So far in my lifetime, I have not yet still experienced riding a boat, nor a ship. That is why I would want my first time experience to be done outside the country.
