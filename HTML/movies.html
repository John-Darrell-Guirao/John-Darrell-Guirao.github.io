TV Shows

One Piece

The first time I discovered One Piece was when I was a grade-schooler. My older cousin always asked me to watch it with him but I tend to immediately refuse. Not when I was in junior high school how I saw myself being addicted to the series as well. For me, One Piece delivered an anime that shows the value of brotherhood, true friendship, unfaltering determination, and many more takeaways that I still hold onto this day.

Gintama

For me, this is the only anime that successfully switch a funny, comedic scene into a serious, hot-confrontation battle. They are also the only anime that I could think of that openly mimiked and mocked other animes which is a pretty uncommon thing to do in the animation industry. Although there are some vulgar and comedic scenes, I will always be amazed on how it keeps me from the edge of my seat once things get serious.

Movies

Kimetsu no Yaiba the Movie:
Mugen Train

The fluid-like animations, delivery of their lines, and the roller coaster of emotions that you would feel while watching this film will surprise you. The Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba series and the animation studio, Studio Ufotable, never fails to deliver the high quality of animation and cinematography to its audiences. That is why, this is one of those remarkable films that you would never get tired from watching it countless of times.

Howl's Moving Castle

This is one of the classic animated films of Studio Ghibli which captured the hearts of many people back in 2004 and up to the present day whenever you show it to the people that will watch it. All of the details, from the animation, plot, music, and even the magical/fantasy vibes that you do not typically see on an actual fantasy film, all of these were the common reasons why many people love this film, including myself.
